## What we build

We're a small team building tools that make it easy to track scores, run competitions, and motivate people — whether that's a youth soccer league, a sales team, or a judged competition.

Our products are used by thousands of organizations every day, from community sports leagues to Fortune 500 companies.

---

### [KeepTheScore.com](https://keepthescore.com)

An online scoreboard and leaderboard tool built for simplicity. Create a live scoreboard in seconds — no account required. Used by sports coaches, teachers, event organizers, and community leagues to track and display scores in real time.

### [Leaderboarded.com](https://leaderboarded.com)

A leaderboard platform for business and professional use. Embed live leaderboards in your intranet, run sales contests, track team KPIs, and recognize top performers — without spreadsheets.

### [ScoreJudge.com](https://scorejudge.com)

A scoring platform for judged competitions and events. Collect scores from multiple judges, aggregate results in real time, and display rankings to participants and audiences.
